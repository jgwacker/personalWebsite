Great-Power Competition in AI

Artificial intelligence has joined the short list of technologies that organize world politics. The chips that train frontier models, the machines that make those chips, and the electricity that runs the data centers are concentrated in a handful of countries and firms. That concentration converts a commercial technology into a strategic one. This guide reads the 2026 AI landscape through the realist tradition: who holds leverage, who depends on whom, and what each state has done about it.
