International Cooperation on AI

Almost every government claims AI requires international rules. Few agree on which rules, who drafts them, or what holds when a major power exits. This guide traces the institutionalist answer: the summits, treaties, voluntary commitments, and standards bodies through which states have tried to govern AI together since 2023 — and the points at which that architecture has begun to come apart.
