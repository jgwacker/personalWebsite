Contested Norms in AI

The international politics of AI is, in the constructivist reading, a contest over norms: which uses of the technology will count as legitimate, and whose vocabulary the rest of the world will adopt to say so. This guide traces five fronts where that contest is now visible — from election interference and content provenance to the human-rights regime, the Vatican, and indigenous data sovereignty.
