<p>
We study extensions of the Standard Model where the Higgs boson dominantly decays via a cascade to four tau leptons, and discuss whether this decay is visible at the Tevatron. We find that with an integrated luminosity of 6 fb<sup>-1</sup>, there can be excesses in multi-lepton events in several channels for a Higgs boson of mass ~ 110 GeV.
</p>
