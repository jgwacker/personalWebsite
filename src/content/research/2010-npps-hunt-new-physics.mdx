<h2>The Scope</h2>

<p>
A 230-page review with over 80 authors, covering every major class of new physics that the LHC could discover: supersymmetry, extra dimensions, extended gauge sectors, hidden sectors, new sources of CP violation, the nature of electroweak symmetry breaking, and TeV-scale string models. Written to serve as a theoretical companion to the experimental Technical Design Reports from ATLAS and CMS, providing the physics motivation for the search program those detectors were built to execute.
</p>
