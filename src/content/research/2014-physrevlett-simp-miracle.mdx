abstract: Introduces the SIMP paradigm in which the thermal relic abundance is set by 3→2 self-annihilations in a hidden sector that remains in kinetic equilibrium with the Standard Model. Predicts light (MeV–GeV) dark matter with sizable self-interactions.
tags: [dark matter, SIMP, thermal relic, phenomenology]

---

Core idea: thermal freeze-out dominated by 3→2 within the dark sector, with elastic scattering maintaining kinetic equilibrium with the SM at freeze-out. Consequences include enhanced self-interactions and modified small-scale structure signatures.
